ABOUT US




OUR VISION

Since their debut in the mid-2010s, the popularity of commercial virtual reality games has skyrocketed. Our team is highly interested in the immersive and engaging nature of virtual and augmented reality environments which have proven to be so captivating for players. Because of this interest, we wanted to dedicate our time in this class to developing our own virtual reality game. Our goal is to create a unique game with equal focus on gameplay, storyline, and aesthetics that will provide users with a full and well-rounded gameplay experience that they will not find anywhere else in the virtual reality game market.

Oculus Rift headset and controllers






WHAT IS VR?

The graphic to the left, created by Julia Tokareva, describes some of the main diferences between VR, AR, and MR. These are three similar and yet different user experiences which are often confused with one another.


As you can see, according to the graphic, VR is a fully immersive, fully artificial environment which the user is placed into. This provides for a totally unique and surreal experience for the player as they feel that they have been placed in a completely new, other-worldly setting that is now theirs to explore.


Our goal is to take advantage of the magic and fascination that VR habitats can provide to players to create a one-of-a-kind world for anyone to conquer.
